## Style Prompt

Create a refined, native-feeling product promo for ChronoChat, a ChatGPT browser extension for navigating long conversations. The look should feel close to ChatGPT: dark graphite surfaces, soft off-white typography, restrained green accent, real browser screenshots, and calm precision. The video should feel useful and focused rather than loud or startup-generic.

## Colors

- Background: `#101010`
- Surface: `#171717`
- Raised surface: `#212121`
- Text primary: `#F4F4F2`
- Text muted: `#B8B8B4`
- Accent: `#10A37F`

## Typography

- Primary: OpenAI Sans, loaded locally from `assets/chronochat/*.woff2`
- Use bold, compact headlines and regular body copy.

## Motion

- Smooth, practical motion: slide, scale, fade, and subtle ambient depth.
- Keep pacing brisk across four scenes in 12 seconds.
- Use the real product screenshot as the main proof point.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon, purple-blue gradients, or flashy SaaS decoration.
- Do not make the UI look detached from ChatGPT.
- Do not invent claims beyond README and manifest features.
- Do not obscure the screenshot with heavy overlays.
